Guided Imagery
Transport yourself mentally to a peaceful place.
  • Sit or lie in a comfortable position
  • Close your eyes and imagine a calming setting like the beach or a peaceful forest
  • Focus on details: What do you hear? Smell? Feel?
  • Breathe slowly and stay in that space for 5 to 10 minutes
Used to reduce tension and restore calm (Slide 30).
Progressive Muscle Relaxation
Helps release built-up physical tension.
  • Start at your toes and work your way up
  • Tense each muscle group (feet, legs, stomach, hands, etc.) for 5 seconds, then relax for 10
  • Focus on the difference between tension and release
Effective for stress and body awareness (Slide 32).
Meditation (Focusing Attention)
Calms the racing mind and reduces anxiety.
  • Sit comfortably and focus on your breath or a single word like ‚Äúpeace‚Äù
  • Let thoughts come and go, do not chase them
  • Start with 5 minutes daily and increase gradually
Meditation reduces anxiety, improves sleep, and promotes emotional health (Slide 29).
